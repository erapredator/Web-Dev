Vegetarian Stir-Fry

  • Time: 1.5 hours
  • Serves: water
Ingredients Quantity
Tofu 210 gr
Broccoli 120 gr
Carrots 100 gr

Step 1: Press tofu to remove excess water, cut into cubes, and stir-fry until golden brown.

Step 2: Add sliced carrots, broccoli florets, and assorted bell peppers to the pan.

Step 3: Mix minced ginger and garlic with soy sauce, then pour over the vegetables and tofu

Step 4: Stir-fry until the vegetables are tender yet crisp.

Step 5: Serve the stir-fry over cooked rice.

Step 1: Season chicken breast with salt and pepper, grill until fully cooked, and slice into strips.

Step 3: Drizzle olive oil and balsamic vinaigrette over the salad, tossing gently to coat
